FAQ

Answers to common questions

Is this like WaniKani, but for hanzi?

Exactly the idea — Chinese characters with a premium, focused experience and an SRS tuned for retention without overwhelm.

Which characters and order?

Core curriculum follows HSK + frequency, with radicals first, then high‑utility characters, then compounds.

Can I create custom decks?

Yes. Tag by textbook, topic, or frequency and mix with the main deck. Pro adds advanced filters and imports.

Does it support traditional characters?

Yes — toggle simplified/traditional per deck; mappings and variants are handled automatically.
